About Steck Care Limited

Our mission is to assist every Service user with improving their quality of life, encouraging independence and allowing them to be comfortable with excellent care in their own home by providing first-class person-centred care with respect compassion of the highest ethical standards.


To ensure that each service user’s values and needs are respected in matters of religion, culture, race or ethnic origin, Marital status, political affiliation, parenthood, disabilities and impairments. To respect the individuality of the service, provide a service that fully satisfies the needs and expectations of the individuals in their own home. To actively seek and value the contribution made by informal carers, families and relevant multi-disciplinary team that are involved in the care planning. To respond positively to feedback from our service users, their families and carer, other health and social care professionals and our staff.


To manage the care service efficiently and effectively, to make the best use of resources and to maximise the best value for both the provider and the service user. To undertake a risk assessment of environment Health and Safety hazards within the service user home and ensure that any hazards are reported to the service user/purchaser. To ensure that all service users are provide with a written information on the organisation’s procedure for handling complaints, comments, complements and how to use it. To ensure all staff are up to date with in house and external training.
