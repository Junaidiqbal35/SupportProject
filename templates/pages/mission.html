Mission

MISSION

To assist every Service user with improving their quality of life, encouraging independence and allowing them to be comfortable with excellent care in their own home by providing first-class person-centred care with respect compassion of the highest ethical standards.


Welcome

Steck Care Limited is based in Oxfordshire, we provide a Bespoke of High Quality and Personal centred care services to the Young Adults, Elderly and Vulnerable people in Oxfordshire. We aim to help improve quality of life and promote health and wellbeing. We are compassionate about having a friendly approach to care that is provided to our service users.

The company was founded in 2019 by Albert Steck, as we have an in-depth knowledge of the social care system due to working with Elderly care, Mental Health and learning disability. Michael has worked in the armed forces and has a lot of experience in business management.

Values

Respect and professionalism: Treat all people supported by us and all people who work here with respect as all times. Respect individual requirement for privacy and all time and treat all information relating to the person in a confidential manner.
